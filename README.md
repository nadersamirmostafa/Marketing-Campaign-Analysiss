## 📌 Project Overview
This project is an **end-to-end data analysis case study** based on business requirements from:

- Marketing Manager  
- Customer Experience Manager  

### Objective:
Analyze marketing performance and customer feedback to identify root causes behind:
- Declining engagement  
- Low conversion rates  
- Reduced customer satisfaction  

Then deliver **data-driven, actionable recommendations**.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
Two main challenges were identified:

1. 📉 Decreased marketing engagement & low conversion rates  
2. 😟 Decline in customer satisfaction based on reviews  

### The task included:
- Data cleaning & transformation  
- KPI engineering  
- Root cause analysis  
- Delivering executive-level insights

### 😊 Customer Experience KPIs

---

## 🔎 Key Insights
- 📉 **Engagement & Conversion Decline**  
  Clear drop over time → weak targeting & audience mismatch  

- 📊 **Channel Performance Gap**  
  High traffic ≠ high revenue → inefficient channels  

- 💬 **Customer Sentiment Issues**  
  Repeated negative feedback impacting retention  

- 💰 **ROI Inefficiency**  
  Marketing spend not optimized  

- ⚠️ **Churn Risk Signals**  
  Negative reviews strongly linked to customer drop-off

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 📈 Improve Conversion
- Leverage 840 satisfied customers as promoters  
- Focus budget on high-conversion products (15.46%)  

### 📢 Boost Engagement
- Shift to short-form video content  
- Focus on social media channels  

### ⭐ Enhance Customer Experience
- Investigate low-rating markets (e.g., Switzerland – 3.49)  
- Actively resolve negative feedback (1–2 star reviews)
